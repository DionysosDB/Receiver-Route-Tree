🏆 Receiver Fundamentals

  • Run FULL SPEED to your break point — never slow down early
  • Plant hard on your outside foot before breaking inside, vice versa
  • Sell every route like it's a deep ball — beat the defender first
  • After your cut, eyes to QB immediately for the ball
  • Catch with your hands, not your body — soft hands win games!
